Fresh Mango Orzo Salad by Souhail Sabri

This mango orzo salad recipe by Souhail Sabri combines sweet diced mangoes, cold orzo, seeded plum tomatoes, scallions, flat leaf parsley, honey, lemon juice, fresh ginger, salt, and pepper.

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1 lb package of orzo, such as Barilla
  • Optional swap: Banza High Protein Gluten Free Chickpea Orzo Pasta
  • 3 cups diced mangoes
  • 5 plum tomatoes, seeded and diced
  • 1 cup scallions, chopped
  • 1 cup chopped flat leaf parsley

Salad Dressing

  • 1/3 cup honey, or more to taste
  • Juice of 3 lemons, or more to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh ginger, grated
  • Optional: lime juice to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the orzo according to the package directions.
  2. Drain the orzo and allow it to cool completely. This salad is best with cold orzo.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, diced mangoes, scallions, parsley, and cooked cold orzo.
  4. Make the salad dressing by mixing lemon juice, honey, grated fresh ginger, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add the dressing to the salad and toss until evenly combined.
  6. Adjust lemon juice and honey to taste depending on the sweetness and size of the mangoes.
  7. Add lime juice if you want a brighter citrus flavor.

Recipe Tips

Always adjust the lemon juice and honey to taste. The final flavor depends on the sweetness, ripeness, and size of the mangoes.

For a gluten-free or higher-protein version, swap regular orzo for Banza High Protein Gluten Free Chickpea Orzo Pasta.
