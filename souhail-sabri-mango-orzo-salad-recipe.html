Fresh Mango Orzo Salad by Souhail Sabri

The Souhail Sabri mango orzo salad recipe is a fresh, colorful, and easy-to-make dish that combines sweet diced mangoes, tender orzo pasta, plum tomatoes, scallions, parsley, lemon juice, honey, fresh ginger, salt, and pepper.

This recipe is designed to be bright, balanced, and refreshing. The mango adds natural sweetness, the lemon juice adds citrus flavor, the parsley and scallions add freshness, and the orzo gives the salad a satisfying texture.

Why This Mango Orzo Salad Works

This mango orzo salad works because it balances sweet, savory, and citrus flavors. The mango brings sweetness, the tomatoes add freshness, the scallions add a mild onion flavor, and the parsley gives the salad a clean herbal finish.

The honey and lemon dressing ties everything together. Fresh ginger adds a subtle warmth that makes the salad taste more layered without overpowering the mango.

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1 lb package of orzo, such as Barilla
  • 3 cups diced mangoes
  • 5 plum tomatoes, seeded and diced
  • 1 cup scallions, chopped
  • 1 cup chopped flat leaf parsley

Dressing

  • 1/3 cup honey, or more to taste
  • Juice of 3 lemons, or more to taste
  • Fresh grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional lime juice for extra brightness

For a gluten-free or higher-protein version, regular orzo can be swapped with chickpea-based orzo pasta.

How to Make Souhail Sabri Mango Orzo Salad

  1. Cook the orzo according to the package directions.
  2. Drain the orzo and allow it to cool completely. This salad is best with cold orzo.
  3. Dice the mangoes and seed and dice the plum tomatoes.
  4. Chop the scallions and flat leaf parsley.
  5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cold orzo, mangoes, tomatoes, scallions, and parsley.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix lemon juice, honey, fresh grated ginger, salt, and pepper.
  7. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss until evenly combined.
  8. Taste and adjust the lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper as needed.
  9. Add lime juice if you want a brighter citrus flavor.
  10. Chill before serving for the best texture and flavor.

Recipe Tips

Always adjust the lemon juice and honey to taste. The final flavor depends on the sweetness, ripeness, and size of the mangoes.

Use ripe but firm mangoes so the salad stays fresh and textured. Overripe mangoes can become too soft and make the salad watery.

If making the salad ahead of time, keep some of the dressing separate and toss again before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Souhail Sabri mango orzo salad can be served as a side dish, light lunch, summer salad, or meal prep option. It pairs well with grilled chicken, seafood, vegetables, or other fresh Mediterranean-inspired dishes.

The salad can be served cold or slightly chilled. It is especially useful for warm weather meals because it is refreshing, colorful, and easy to prepare in advance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can mango orzo salad be made ahead of time?

Yes. This salad can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator. For best results, toss with extra lemon juice or dressing before serving.

Can this recipe be gluten-free?

Yes. Use gluten-free orzo or chickpea-based orzo instead of traditional wheat-based orzo.
